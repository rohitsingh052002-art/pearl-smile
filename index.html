Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit to PearlSmile Dental Clinic.

We understand dental anxiety completely. Dr. Anushka uses the latest pain-management techniques, including topical numbing gels before injections, gentle instrumentation, and sedation options for highly anxious patients. Most procedures are virtually painless with modern techniques.

We recommend a professional check-up and cleaning every 6 months for most adults. If you have specific dental conditions such as gum disease, cavities, or are undergoing orthodontic treatment, more frequent visits may be needed.

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans and no-cost EMI options through leading banks for treatments like implants, aligners, and veneers. Our billing team will help you choose the most convenient option during your consultation.

Professional teeth whitening at our clinic typically lasts between 1–3 years depending on your diet and oral hygiene habits. Avoiding tea, coffee, and tobacco, combined with proper brushing, helps maintain results much longer.

Orthodontic treatment is most effective when started between ages 10–14 as the jaw is still developing. However, adults can benefit from braces or clear aligners at any age. Dr. Anushka offers treatments for children, teens, and adults.

Absolutely! In fact, the American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry recommends the first dental visit by age 1 or when the first tooth erupts. Early visits help detect problems early and help children build a positive association with dental care.
